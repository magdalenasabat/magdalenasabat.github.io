## Descritpion
Organisers: [Anne Urai](https://anneurai.net) (Leiden University) & [Stefano Palminteri](https://sites.google.com/site/stefanopalminteri/) (ENS)

Biologically informed models of decision making applied to sustainability and climate action.

We envision this meeting will bring together researchers from cognitive (neuro)science, social psychology, behavioral science and social modelling to explore joint interests and (inter)national collaborative networks.
